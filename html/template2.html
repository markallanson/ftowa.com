  June 8th, 2002 - MC Motorsport Day

Just a quick note to thank you all for the great participation at MC Motorsport.

Personal highlights for me...

  • Every single person who confirmed rolled up on the day. To get 12 out of 12 attending without a single no-show really made my day.
  • Everyone stayed safe and drove home at the end of the day. No mechanical problems, tow-ways, etc.
  • John Clark commenting to me that "This is a really good group of people", He also asked if we were a car club, and was pretty astonished that we were just an informal bunch.

Seeing everyone really get in there and give it a real go. They'd mentioned beforehand that for groups scared to pull their finger out and push things to their limits, the instructors often get the fire hose out and wet the corners down (to induce understeer and oversteer for timid drivers). As you probably noticed, they weren't looking to get out any fire hoses for us! :)

I'll get the pictures up asap, and let you know where they are. I've attached one now to whet your appetite. If anyone has a good shot of my off-road adventure, can you email it to me please. My wife thinks it is very funny, and would like to make fun of me some more. If I'd done the same thing off a road up a kerb, she probably wouldn't be so amused.

Thanks again! I'll be in touch about the next proposed group outing, whatever that may be.

- Rich
