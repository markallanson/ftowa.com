Do you have an alarm/immobiliser that might be (a) malfunctioning, or (b) be badly installed so that the unplugging of ECU wiring might have disturbed patch leads to and from the immobiliser?

The original engine dying bit sounds (from the little you've described) like an Idle Speed Controller (stepper motor) symptom.

This might be a job for a professional! But take another look at that wiring near the engine ECU and see if something got pulled, shifted, etc.

Good luck!
