Now that I've had my car for a few months, I need to get some cosmetic stuff sorted out with it. The main problem I'm having is deciding what to do with the rims that came with the car.

Here are some pics of what it looks like right now (each pic is around 400kB so you can see all the details):

Whole car image
Image of rims

As you can see, things aren't looking too pretty right now (and yes, I know there's a nut missing on that front wheel - it had rusted on and I'd just removed it).

I've had mixed opinions about the red rims, ranging from "awesome, makes the car to stand out" to "godawful, change them asap". I'm kind of in the middle of the road with the issue. I like the individuality that they bring to the car, but they're in such a shocking state that I'm wondering if they're worth salvaging. My main issue is that they look so small, even though they are 17 inchers.

As far as I see it, my options are as follows:

1) Fix up the current rims. Keep the red theme going and possible expand it to the large black outer wall that doesn't really serve much function other than to look completely dull. Buy some new capping for the rims to cover the nuts and generally make the whole centre of the wheel look better (any ideas where I could go to get something like that? I can't find any brand names as such so I wouldn't know what company to go through). On the other side there's some evidence of kerb kissing that has gone on from the previous owner (read: there's chunks out of the black outer section). Is there a way that it can be ground back / filled in to look decent again? This would be the cheapest option by far, and more favourable to me as I'm just a poor uni student!

2) Find some stock rims to replace the current ones. Are there any yards or wreckers that would have a set or am I chasing something unattainable? This option is viable providing I can source enough funds.

3) Buy some completely new rims - maybe go some 18 inchers and some wafer thin-walled low profiles. Basically not an option, but I can dream, can't I?

What do you guys think? I'm basically looking for feedback on options 1 & 2. Ideally I'd like to get something sorted out before the photoshoot and not be subjected to international scrutiny as a result of my rims. If you took the time to read all this, thankyou!
