Stop thinking so much! You'll strain something!!!

> only equal to a integra vti-r..???

Lots of factors go into this...

- How old is the car, and how well has it been looked after? Are you comparing a 10 year old car (that may need a major service) against a near-new car in peak condition?

- Is your boot full of ICE gear? Is your mate's?


> tappet problem, can that really be taking that much away from the engine?

A few newton metres of torque. Nothing big.


> i also heard that a CAI would help alot...

Here's one I made earlier!...

http://www.ftowa.com/html/workshop/workshopart10.html


> are they just talking shit...?

Welcome to the net!!!


> there was also a few times when i rev over the red line and into the
> red "danger" zone, and the bloody engine kinda cut on me..? is this
> a common problem?

That's your rev limiter. It stops you blowing up your engine. Which is A Good Thing.


> what rev range do you guys shift on...?

Change at 8,000rpm. 1->2 and 2->3 definitely. Don't really care about 3->4 or 4->5... I'm never in a position to need to know the "perfect shift point" that fast.

Make sure you are treating your engine nicely if you insist on doing this with any regularity. Old drivebelts, old timing belt, or crap oil will cut short your fun with a bang!


> the damn fto gear ratios are too small in 1st....lol

Nearly 60km/h in first gear isn't too shabby.

> 0.5 secs in to the damn thing and whoof
> i have to shift, no advantage of mivec....

It's because of the usable torque from 5500rpm to 8000rpm due to the MIVEC cams and timing that you're up at redline so fast, and changing to 2nd!



Ah, you need to stop racing your mate's Integra and come and do some motorkhana training. If you like, we can book in a couple of FTOs (including mine, hehe) and you can get that fix of adrenaline... and learn some good stuff along the way!!

- Rich
