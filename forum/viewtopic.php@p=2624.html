There is a very detailed 'sticky' posting up at the UK FTO Owners club forum.

Go to http://www.ftooc.org/contents/, select 'Forum', then find the Gearbox section.

One of the top posts is entitled: Sticky: Tiptronic Flashing "N"

Check it out.


Also, under their 'General' section, there is a similarly excellent summary called: Sticky: Error codes and their diagnosis


These both go further than the "how to extract the codes" article on our site.


Hope this helps.


Incidentally, the FTO's INVECS-II tiptronic gearbox is no longer a mystery for Mitsubishi dealerships... Magnas have had this type of gearbox for years now. You shouldn't be faced with blank looks if you take it to a competent dealership and ask for an opinion. Of course, dealer service centres ain't usually known for being cost-effective, hehe.


- Rich
