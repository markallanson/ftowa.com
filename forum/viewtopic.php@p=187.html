Posted: Mon Mar 24, 2003 8:37 am    Post subject: Throttle Bodies
The throttle bodies can be machined out to eitehr 63 or 64mm depending upon the thickness of the core we are working with. Sicne they are a casting like anything else, there can be differences in how thick it is and that is what determines the difference in the diameter we machine it out to.

Either way 63 or 64mm, there is still the same percantage gain.

With regards to FPR units -we beleive they are necessary on the FTO vehicles due to the fact they run a MAP sensor and are not senstive to changes in air flow so the minuite you start modding the car and increasing air flow - the car does not recognise this and feeds teh same fuel supply hence the car starts to run lean and then loses power.

We found our extractors especially leaned the car out a lot hence why we recomend the FPR unit with any extractor upgrade.
