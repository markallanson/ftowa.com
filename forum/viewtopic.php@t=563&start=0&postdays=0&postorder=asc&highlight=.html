try this amp out at your local car stereo dealer if they have it.. since ur getting an alpine maybe this model might be at the reasonable range.
the model is of Alpine V12 MRV_T301 300 rms wattage 2 channel. that is if ur type r sub is 10 ". if say ur sub is about 12" then i'll suggest u to get an amp of around 500 to 700 rms.
