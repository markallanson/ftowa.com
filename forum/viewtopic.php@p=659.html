Car depreciates at 18.75% as of 1 July 2002. That is the rate insurance company would use if you're insuring at market value. It was previously set at 22.5% prior to July 2002.

If it doesn't cost you an arm and leg, it would be best to insure at agreed value. Generally the GPX are around $18k - $21k - check the newspaper or auto trader for price guide.

I had mine agreed on $27K, but if I was insured at market value, it would probably be between $13k to $15k if it was a write off. Man!!! With $15K I can't even buy myself a GPX.

Hope that helps

Fred
