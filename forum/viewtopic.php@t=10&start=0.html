Regarding Bunbury, Jeff Crisdale has put together this gem of a plan...

---

Have you ever driven the Bunbury - Donnybrook -Mummbellup - Collie - Burekup circuit around Wellington Dam?

Nice drive indeed. Lots of hills, hidden valleys..lovely forest...pretty good quality roads and lots and lots of sweeping curves with not much traffic. It would be a full day trip though (approx. 550km round trip to Perth) with an itinerary something like:

1. Leave early - say 6.30am (is that too early?) from meeting place in the carpark at Gateways Shopping Centre near the intersection of Kwinana Fwy and Armadale Rd.
2. Easy 40km drive down Fwy to Mandurah (1st pit stop about 7.15am).
3. Leave Mandurah approx. 7.30am and continue down Old Coast Rd to Bunbury (approx. 105km) for well deserved morning tea in the Cafe strip (about 9.00-9.30am)
4. Cruise around Bunbury City sights (I have a few spots in mind - the lookout and lighthouse area for one and the beachfront another - for approx.1 hour. (say 9.30 - 10.30)
5. Head 36km to Donnybrook (arrive approx. 11am) - 15 min pit stop.
6. Drive 29km east to Mummballup then 21km north to Collie - LUNCH stop. (approx.12noon to 1.00pm)
7. Drive west to Wellington dam (arrive approx. 1.30pm) for sightseeing (approx. 45mins) Leave approx. 2.15pm.
8. Drive back along SW Hwy (approx. 110km) to Pinjarra Central Park (late afternoon tea approx. 4.00pm-4.30pm)
9. Return 86km to Perth approx. 5.45-6.00pm. Celebrate safe return!

Hehe. What do you think? Is that too far for one day of "cruising"?

---

I'm certainly up for it!

- Rich
