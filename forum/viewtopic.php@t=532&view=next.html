This does in fact sound like the engine ECU enforcing a low rev limiter.

Has the alternator been rebuilt or replaced? If so, it could have been done wrong. There is apparently a signal from the alternator to the engine ECU (an extra pinout on the GPX compared to most alternators), and if something's not right it will limit.

It could also be another sensor issue. There is an easy way to extract error codes - see the 'Workshop' article here...

http://www.ftowa.com/html/workshop/workshopart07.html

That will most likely point you in the right direction.

- Rich
