Yes, it's definitely on!

As emailed to registered people:

---------------

Graeme Bassula has very kindly organised an FTO drag racing session on the
night of January 14th at Quit Motorplex... commonly known as "Whoop-Ass
Wednesday"!

Full details are available at http://www.ftowa.com/html/calendar.html, but
briefly (as stated on the Motorplex website)...

"Whoop Ass Wednesday Night Street Racing is just the thing for those wishing
to experience the thrill of Drag Racing without the pressure of competition.
All you need to compete is a licenced/muffled Street car or bike, a helmet
and long sleeves, long pants and enclosed footware. You can race your car
or bike as many times as you wish and you will find the staff on hand only
too willing to ensure that your first experience at Drag Racing is an
enjoyable one."

Cost will be $30.00 for as many runs as you like on the night. See the
'Events' page for spectator/family pricing.

This has been discussed for a couple of weeks on the Forum, so we have a few
likely attendees already. These are:

Vlad
Richard H
Graeme B
Peter T
Paul E
Leigh B

If you would like to be added, please drop me an email. This list will be
updated regularly on the 'Events' page at www.ftowa.com.

So come on down to the 'plex, line up against your fellow FTO drivers and
have a bit of good-natured fun.

---------------

- Rich
