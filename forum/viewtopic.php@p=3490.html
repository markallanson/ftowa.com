G'day! Long time no see...

Give a Mitsubishi dealer a ring, and ask about those part numbers (MR252755 or MR252756). They are likely to be in other locally sold cars, so carried as spares. Even if you're not likely to go with their extortionate new-part price tag, you might find which other cars use it. Which would be useful info when ringing wreckers!

Or subscribe to partslocator.com.au and do a search for FTO stuff. Don't expect it to be up-to-date though - just use it as a quick lookup of Oz wreckers that might be FTO-aware.

Good luck...
