That's weird. That is a GPX engine, but it looks like a GR air cleaner box and induction pipe. There is a little box on top of the airway leading out of the air cleaner. That's not a GPX fitting.

The GPX uses MAP (manifold absolute pressure) sensor for the engine management, which is just a wee pipe off the manifold to a little unit way at the back. The air box has a nice big exit pipe, without any of that extra plastic stuff.

That little box looks to be the MAS (mass airflow sensor) from the non-MIVEC engine.

The engine is certainly MIVEC - the oil filler is on the left, etc., so no worries there.

I would say this used to have a pod filter fitted, but they have taken it off and put on the nearest thing they could find to a stock looking induction pipe. Not the right one for the car, though. I can't see it being a huge deal in isolation, but it could indicate the car has seen some thrashing and tweaking in its life. Then again, so's mine!!!

The only other items of note are the aftermarket pedals and steering wheel. So it's not been a stock "church on sundays" FTO then, hehe.

Hope that helps!
